Anonymization Checklist
Run every draft against this before it's committed.
No employer name, parent company name, or industry-specific identifiers that narrow it to one company
No exact user counts, exact team size, or exact dates — use ranges or relative terms ("a multi-thousand-user environment," "over several months")
No internal tool names, ticket IDs, hostnames, or system identifiers beyond the named platform itself (Delinea, Oracle Fusion)
No org chart details — titles and reporting lines stay out
No screenshots or diagrams pulled from the real environment; redraw generic versions if a visual is needed
No metrics that could be reverse-searched to a specific public incident, audit, or press mention
Read it as a stranger with no context — if anything lets them guess the employer, cut it
